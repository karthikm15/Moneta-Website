WHAT WE DO

Moneta strives to provide services to increase financial literacy among high school students.

Articles

Every week, different articles are published on Medium, discussing ways to optimize financial success and highlighting different sections of finance (e.g. budgeting, etc.)

Podcasts

Podcasts with reputed people in the financial field and with individuals who have gone through grueling financial struggles will be featured so that we can learn more about their experiences.

Website

Our website not only provides an overview of our articles and podcasts but also provides a space to find our latest ventures. Our website hosts constant support for those who have questions.

Upkey

Moneta is partnered with Upkey, a career readiness company that promotes financial literacy. With Upkey, Moneta plans to provide a widespread platform for all individuals.

Updates

Moneta constantly updates their website through their articles and podcasts to provide the most up-to-date and relevant information about what is currently going on in the financial world.

Outreach

Moneta constantly works to expand its network of financial experts and innovative high schools students to provide a community of like-minded individuals to bounce ideas and suggestions.
